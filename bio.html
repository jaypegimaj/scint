About Me

My name is Susan Clouse, I'm a certified interior designer based in the San Francisco Bay area. I was a software developer for 20 years before I decided to pursue my passion for interior design, and have both an AKBD Degree in Interior Design and BA in Computer Science.

Welcome to Susan Clouse Interior Solutions where you will find elegant, timeless and innovative solutions to your interior design needs.

Whether you need the perfect final touch to complete a room or, are starting out with a blank canvas, Susan's extensive design knowledge and expertise can help you to achieve a beautiful and comfortable environment.

Susan is a certified interior designer based in the San Francisco Bay area. She left her 20 year software development career to pursue her passion in interior design. She has a degree in Computer Science and in Interior Design.

She likes helping people create a satisfying and enjoyable living environment. Her numerous satisfied customers can attest to her superb sense of coordination, creativity, effective use of color and texture and efficient organization that can transform any area into a beautiful and functional living space. She takes personal satisfaction in seeing people content with their surroundings.

Susan Clouse Interior Solutions respects your personal space and individual needs and is committed to make the design process a pleasurable experience for everyone involved. Susan Clouse, AKBD
